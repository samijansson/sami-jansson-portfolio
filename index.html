I've spent over a decade working at the intersection of quality, automation, and the tools that make software teams more effective. What drives me isn't the technology itself — it's seeing a team go from manual, error-prone processes to something that just works, reliably and at scale.

Whether I'm architecting an automation framework from scratch, migrating a platform used by hundreds of people, or sitting down with a test team to understand where things are breaking down — I bring the same approach: listen first, understand the real problem, then build something that lasts.
